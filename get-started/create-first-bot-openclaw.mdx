---

Creating a bot in Agent 37 gives you a hosted OpenClaw instance that you can configure, manage, and connect to channels.

<Info>
  **Before you begin**:
  - Access to the [Agent 37 dashboard](https://www.agent37.com/dashboard)
  - A plan with available bot capacity
</Info>

## Create a bot from the dashboard

<Steps>
<Step title="Sign in to Agent 37">
  Open the [Agent 37 dashboard](https://www.agent37.com/dashboard) and sign in to your workspace.
</Step>

<Step title="Click Create Bot">
  In the top-right corner of the dashboard, click **Create Bot**.
</Step>

<Step title="Wait for provisioning">
  Agent 37 will create a hosted instance for your bot. When setup finishes, the bot will appear in your instance list.
</Step>

<Step title="Open the bot row">
  Find your new bot in the list and review its tier, status, and available actions.
</Step>
</Steps>

## What you can do next

Once your bot exists, you can:

- Open **Terminal** to run OpenClaw commands
- Open **Manage** to review settings
- Open **Web Chat** to test conversations
- Connect channels like WhatsApp or Telegram

<Card
  title="Connect your first channel"
  icon="plug"
  href="/get-started/connect-first-channel-openclaw"
>
  Continue to the next setup step and link your bot to a real messaging channel.
</Card>

<Note>
  If the bot does not appear right away, refresh the dashboard and wait until the
  status shows that the instance is ready.
</Note>
