## **Step 1: Access the Agent37 Dashboard**

Start by visiting the Agent37 dashboard:

🔗 [https://www.agent37.com/dashboard](https://www.agent37.com/dashboard)

Log in to your account. If you don’t have one yet, create an account to get started.

## **Step 2: Create a New Instance**

Once logged in:

1. Navigate to your dashboard.
2. Click on **Create Instance**.
3. Wait for the instance to be successfully created.

Your instance is essentially the environment where your OpencClaw bot will run.

## **Step 3: Open the Terminal**

After your instance is ready:

1. Click on your newly created instance.
2. Select **Terminal** from the available options.

This will open a command-line interface where you can configure your OpenClaw bot.

## **Step 4: Run the Configuration Command**

Inside the terminal, execute the following command:

```text
openclaw configure
```

This command will initiate the configuration process for your OpenClaw bot, including the WhatsApp setup.

## **Step 5: Gateway Setup**

When prompted:

- **Where will the gateway run?**

👉 Select `Local`

Next prompt:

- **Link WhatsApp Now?**

> 👉 Select `Yes`

<Frame>

## **Step 6: Scan the QR Code**

Now, take your phone and:

1. Open **WhatsApp**
2. Tap the **3-dot menu** (top-right corner)
3. Select **Linked Devices**
4. Tap **Link a Device**
5. Scan the QR code displayed in your terminal

Once scanned successfully, your WhatsApp account will connect to the Openclaw gateway.

<Frame>
  ![Image2](/images/image2.png)
</Frame>

## **Step 7: Confirm Connection**

After scanning the QR code:

👉 Go back to the terminal.

## **Step 8: WhatsApp Phone Setup**

After successfully scanning the QR code and linking your account, you’ll complete the WhatsApp phone setup process. Follow the steps below carefully.

When prompted in the terminal:

- **WhatsApp phone setup**

👉 Select `This is my personal phone number`

<Frame>
  ![Image6](/images/image6.png)
</Frame>

- **You will then be asked to:**

👉 `Enter your phone number`

<Frame>
  ![Image7](/images/image7.png)
</Frame>

Make sure you enter the correct number linked to your WhatsApp account.

## **Step 9: Complete Setup**

After entering your number:

1. Navigate to `Finish`
2. Then select `Continue`

<Frame>
  ![Image1](/images/image1.png)
</Frame>

This completes the WhatsApp channel configuration.

## **Step 10: Test Your Bot**

Now open **WhatsApp** on your phone.

In the search bar:

- Search for **your own number**

Start a chat and send a message.

If everything is configured correctly, your **OpenClaw bot will respond automatically** 🎉
